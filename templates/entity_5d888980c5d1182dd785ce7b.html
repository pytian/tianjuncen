Is the ICRC an NGO?

The International Committee for the Red Cross ( ICRC ) is a unique institution (neither an IGO nor an NGO ) based in Switzerland, which has a mandate to protect the victims of international and internal armed conflicts under the Geneva Conventions and their Additional Protocols.
