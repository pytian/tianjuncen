Hangzhou culture expo kicks off

The 13th Hangzhou Cultural & Creative Industry Expo opened in Hangzhou, East China's Zhejiang province on Thursday and will run until Monday.Over 400 overseas cultural enterprises and institutions from 53 countries and regions are in attendance.As one of China's biggest cultural and creative industry events, this year's gala features eight exhibition areas that cover a total area of 70,000 square meters.
