Online novels seen as 'covert threat' to youths

Research shows lack of oversight on vulgarity, violence on literature sitesOnline games and pornographic websites are widely deemed threats to children's mental wellbeing and have long been in the crosshairs of internet watchdogs, parents and teachers.Online novels, however, have received less scrutiny as they are believed to appeal less to school children.
