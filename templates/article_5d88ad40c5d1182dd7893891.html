Jinan hosts intl conference on spring cultural landscape

The 2019 International Spring Cultural Landscape City Alliance Conference was held in Jinan on Sept 10. Jinan is a city in eastern China's Shandong province noted for its many natural springs, leading to the nickname "City of Springs".The conference focused on cultural heritage protection and sustainable development in the city and sought to connect it with other spring cities around the world."The unique culture and landscape of Jinan, which is integrated with the city's springs, represents a unique type of water resources utilization in China and the world. It is an important reference for respecting nature, rational use of nature and sustainable development," said Sun Shutao, mayor of Jinan.

[Photo/iasic.com.cn]The 2019 International Advanced Semiconductor Innovation Competition (ASIC) Yangtze River Delta Division came to an end in Zhangjiagang, East China's Jiangsu province on Sept 6. Since launched, this year's contest has attracted nearly 70 projects from 18 cities and regions across China, covering fields of the third-generation semiconductor chips, compound semiconductors, solar photovoltaic, intelligent manufacturing, internet of things and new-generation information technologyExperts, business executives and investors from related fields were also invited to make assessment for the entries and a total of 15 items were qualified for the finals. Jointly hosted by China Advanced Semiconductor Industry Innovation Alliance and Zhangjiagang government, the 2019 IASIC Yangtze River Delta Division was designed to provide a platform for enthusiasts to exchange innovative experience in semiconductor.A total of 6 projects stood out and won the first, second and third prizes and gained corresponding rewards.Lu Chongmin, deputy mayor of Zhangjiagang, attended the awarding ceremony and said the city is longing for more innovative talents. Zhangjiagang boasts rich resources in semiconductor industry. The competition will help the port city attract more talents and projects in the fields and further promote its high-quality development.
