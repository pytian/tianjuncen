Magic's 'Dream Team' jersey on the block

A 1992 US Olympic 'Dream Team' jersey worn by Magic Johnson and memorabilia from former New York Knicks coach Red Holzman are among items available in an online auction that began on Wednesday. SCP Auctions launched the bidding process for several items from NBA legends and Major League Baseball greats in the auction, which runs until Sept 28. The item expected to receive the biggest bids is the blue No 15 Team USA jersey worn by Johnson as a member of the first US Olympic collection of NBA stars, which rolled unbeaten through the 1992 Barcelona Olympics.
